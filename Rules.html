TOURNAMENT FORMAT & RULES

  • The tournament will be a Doubles Darts Tournament.
  • Teams will be divided into two groups namely:
    - RANTHAMBORE
    -SUNDARBANS
  • There will be 8 teams, each consisting of two players as partners for doubles.
  • Group
    - RANTHAMBORE will consist of 4 teams
    - SUNDARBANS will consist of 4 teams
  • The names of the teams in both the groups are as following:
    - RANTHAMBORE:
              AMUR
              CORBETT
              SUNDA
              JAWAN
    - SUNDARBANS:
              BENGAL
              BALI
              SUMATRAN
              MALAYAN
  • The participating partners for the teams will be drawn at 3:00 PM on 2nd October 2020 in Mohor Kutir Resort.
  • Each group will have round robin league during which each team will play a match of 5 legs with all the teams within their group. All events will be best of 5 legs. In case, any team wins the match by 3-0 or 3-1, the remaining leg(s) will be of 301 points.
  • Each win will be rewarded with 2 points and the straight win (5-0) will be rewarded with 1 bonus point.  
  • A maximum of 3 minutes will be given to finish a game if both opponent teams are below 10 points. In case a game is not finished even after this then it will be decided by nearest to the bull throw.  
  • A maximum time of 15 seconds will be given to a player to finish each round of throw (3 darts).
  • Top two teams from each group will qualify for Playoffs on the basis of points and Net Legs. Playoffs matches will be of 5 legs each.  
  • In case of a tie for the top two positions in a group, there will be a 501 points 1-leg match, and the winning team will qualify for playoffs.
  • Only 2 warnings will be given during a match if any player crosses the Ouch line while playing, after that nil point will be considered for that pin(s).
